longDescription: Shark Tank is a reality TV show that features aspiring entrepreneurs pitching their business ideas to a panel of wealthy investors, or "sharks," in hopes of securing funding. The show provides insights into business vocabulary, negotiation skills, and presentation techniques. The interactions between the entrepreneurs and investors offer practical examples of persuasive language and professional jargon. It's an excellent resource for learners interested in business English and entrepreneurship, as well as understanding the dynamics of investment and startup culture. Viewers will pick up on key phrases like "For that reason, I'm out" that are well-known among fans.
